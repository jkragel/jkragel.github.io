James Kragel

Postdoctoral Scholar

University of Chicago

I am a postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Neurology at the University of Chicago. My research interests include episodic memory, computational modeling, the hippocampus, eye movements, and brain stimulation.

Research

Memory-guided Visual Exploration
Using simultaneous neural recordings and eye tracking to understand how memory systems influence exploration and learning.
Modulation of Memory Circuits
Using brain stimulation to modulate memory.
Retrieved Context Models
Using computational modeling and neural recordings to understand how the brain influences recall and memory-guided behaviors.
